Add-ons & Extras

Extend your Sealmetrics plan with additional AI questions. All plans already include unlimited websites and unlimited users.

Available Add-ons​

How to Add​

Via Dashboard​

  1. Go to Settings > Billing
  2. Find the Need more Lens AI questions? section
  3. Select the number of packs
  4. Click Buy
  5. Complete checkout

Via Sales​

For custom configurations or volume discounts:

Add-on vs Upgrade​

When to Use Add-ons​

Add-ons are best when:

  • You need more AI questions but your event volume is fine
  • Your needs are temporary

When to Upgrade​

Upgrading is better when:

  • You consistently need more events
  • You need features from a higher tier (BigQuery, webhooks, audit logs)
  • Add-on costs approach the upgrade cost

Question Pack Details​

How Question Packs Work​

  1. Purchase: Buy one or more packs (500 questions each)
  2. Add to balance: Questions add to your available balance
  3. Use: Questions deduct from your monthly allowance first, then from purchased packs
  4. Rollover: Unused pack questions never expire

Checking Balance​

Go to Settings > Billing and view the Lens AI Questions usage in the Usage card.

Frequently Asked Questions​

Do add-ons auto-renew?​

Question packs are one-time purchases and do not auto-renew. You can purchase additional packs whenever you need them.
