Billing​

Prorated Charges​

When you add mid-cycle:

  • First charge is prorated for remaining days
  • Full charge applies on next billing cycle

Example:

Add 10 Account Pack on Jan 15
Monthly price: €300

January charge (prorated): €150 (15/30 days)
February charge (full): €300

Removing Add-ons​

To remove an add-on:

  1. Go to Settings → Billing → Add-ons
  2. Find the add-on to remove
  3. Click Remove
  4. Confirm removal

Important:

  • Add-ons are removed at end of billing cycle
  • No refunds for partial periods
  • Accounts/data over new limits may be affected

Add-on vs Upgrade​

When to Use Add-ons​

Add-ons are best when:

  • You need one specific thing (more accounts, more pageviews)
  • Your needs are temporary (seasonal traffic)
  • Upgrading would include features you don't need

When to Upgrade​

Upgrading is better when:

  • You need multiple features from the higher tier
  • Add-on costs approach the upgrade cost
  • You want included features (SSO, dedicated support)

Cost comparison example:

Current: Research (€199/month)
Need: 3 accounts + 1M extra pageviews

Recommendation: Upgrade to Guard
  - Only €35 more
  - Get 60+ anomaly rules
  - Get BigQuery integration
  - Get real-time alerts

Question Pack Details​

How Question Packs Work​

  1. Purchase: Buy a pack (100, 250, or 500 questions)
  2. Add to balance: Questions add to your available balance
  3. Use: Questions deduct from pack first, then plan allowance
  4. Rollover: Unused pack questions never expire

Usage Order​

Monthly allowance: 200 questions (Guard plan)
Question pack: 100 questions purchased

When you ask a question:
1. First uses pack questions (100 → 99)
2. Then uses monthly allowance (200 → 199)
3. Then charges overage (€0.40/question)

At month end:
- Pack questions: Remaining roll over
- Monthly allowance: Resets to 200

Frequently Asked Questions​

Can I stack multiple add-ons?​

Yes. You can add multiple packs of the same type:

  • 2x Account Packs = 20 extra accounts
  • 3x Pageview Packs = 3M extra pageviews

Do add-ons auto-renew?​

Yes, all subscription add-ons auto-renew with your plan. Question packs are one-time purchases.

Can I downgrade after adding?​

Yes, but:

  • If you have accounts over the new limit, you must archive or delete some
  • If you're over pageview limits, overage charges may apply
  • Remove add-ons before downgrading to avoid conflicts

Are there volume discounts?​

Yes, contact sales@sealmetrics.com for:

  • 20%+ discount on annual add-on commitments
  • Custom pricing for large volumes
  • Bundle pricing for multiple add-ons
