Frequently Asked Questions​

Can I change plans?​

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately:

  • Upgrades: Prorated charge for remaining billing period
  • Downgrades: Credit applied to future invoices

Is there a free trial?​

Yes, all plans include a 14-day free trial with full features. No credit card required to start.

What happens if I exceed my pageview limit?​

You'll receive notifications at 80% and 100% usage. If you exceed:

  • Service continues uninterrupted
  • Overage charged at €0.20 per 10,000 pageviews
  • Option to upgrade to avoid overages

Do you offer discounts?​

  • Annual billing: Save ~17% (2 months free)
  • Nonprofits: 50% discount (contact us)
  • Startups: Special pricing available (contact us)

Can I cancel anytime?​

Yes, cancel anytime with no penalties. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
