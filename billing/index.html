Choosing the Right Plan​

Starter (€229/month, €199 annual)​

Best for: Small e-commerce businesses and startups

Choose Starter if you:

  • Are getting started with privacy-first analytics
  • Have up to 1M monthly events
  • Need all core features without BigQuery

Growth (€459/month, €399 annual)​

Best for: Growing teams that need BigQuery export

Choose Growth if you:

  • Need BigQuery integration for custom analysis
  • Have up to 5M monthly events
  • Want chat support and more AI questions

Scale (€919/month, €799 annual)​

Best for: Multi-brand retailers

Choose Scale if you:

  • Need webhooks and audit logs
  • Have up to 25M monthly events
  • Require advanced role-based access control
  • Need priority support

Pro (€1,724/month, €1,499 annual)​

Best for: Large retail groups

Choose Pro if you:

  • Have up to 100M monthly events
  • Need 99.9% SLA guarantee
  • Want maximum API throughput (960 req/min)
  • Need dedicated onboarding sessions

Enterprise (Custom pricing)​

Best for: Large organizations with custom needs

Choose Enterprise if you:

  • Need unlimited events
  • Require SSO (SAML) integration
  • Want a dedicated account manager
  • Need isolated data processing

Frequently Asked Questions​

Can I change plans?​

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately:

  • Upgrades: Prorated charge for remaining billing period
  • Downgrades: Credit applied to future invoices

Is there a free trial?​

Yes, all plans include a 14-day free trial with full features. A credit card is required at signup, but your card will not be charged during the 14-day trial period.

What happens if I exceed my event limit?​

Tracking continues without interruption. Overage is billed per 100,000 additional events at your plan's overage rate. You'll receive notifications at 80% and 100% usage.

Do you offer discounts?​

  • Annual billing: Save approximately 15% compared to monthly
  • Nonprofits: 50% discount (contact us)
  • Startups: Special pricing available (contact us)

Can I cancel anytime?​

Yes, cancel anytime with no penalties. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
