# Add-ons & Extras

All plans already include unlimited websites and unlimited users, plus full access to LENS AI through your own LLM provider key. The add-ons below cover the remaining custom needs.

**Note:**
LENS AI runs on a **bring-your-own-key (BYOK)** model — you connect your own Anthropic, OpenAI, DeepSeek, or Gemini API key, and your questions are billed directly by that provider. Sealmetrics does **not** impose a monthly question quota, so there is no "question allowance" to top up. See the [AI Assistant docs](/lens/ai-assistant/) for setup.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I pay Sealmetrics extra for LENS AI usage?

No. LENS AI uses a bring-your-own-key model with no monthly quota set by Sealmetrics, so there is nothing to buy or top up — your AI usage is billed by the LLM provider whose key you connect.
