# See Your Data Flow in Real-Time with Sealmetrics

The beauty of modern analytics? **Instant feedback.**  
Here’s how simple it is with Sealmetrics.

---

## The 3-Step Real-Time Magic

1. **Deploy your pixels** — Install the Sealmetrics tracking code on your site  
2. **Enable real-time tracking** — Turn on live monitoring in your dashboard  
3. **Generate events** — Let users interact with your site  

**Result:**  
Your events appear on screen in seconds.  
**Maximum delay:** under 10 seconds.  

---

## Why Real-Time Matters

- **Immediate validation:** Know your tracking works instantly  
- **Live campaign monitoring:** Watch conversions happen as they occur  
- **Faster debugging:** Spot issues before they impact your data  
- **Enhanced user experience:** Personalize content based on live behavior  

---

## The Sealmetrics Difference

While most analytics tools make you wait **minutes or even hours** for data processing, Sealmetrics provides **instant insights** — because in today’s fast-paced digital world, delayed data is often useless data.

**No cookies. No consent banners. No delays.**

Just **pure, real-time, consentless analytics** that works the moment you need it.
