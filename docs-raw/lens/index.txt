# LENS AI

LENS is Sealmetrics' AI-powered analytics assistant that proactively monitors your data, detects anomalies, and provides actionable insights.

## What is LENS?

LENS (Learning Engine for Notifications & Signals) combines rule-based anomaly detection with advanced AI to help you:

- **Detect problems before they impact your business** - Get alerts when traffic drops, conversion rates change, or campaigns underperform
- **Understand your data faster** - Ask questions in natural language and get instant answers
- **Receive automated insights** - Weekly executive summaries and real-time anomaly notifications

## Key Components

### Anomaly Detection

LENS continuously monitors your data using **60+ detection rules** across multiple categories:

| Category | What it monitors |
|----------|------------------|
| Traffic | Unusual spikes or drops in pageviews, sessions, users |
| Conversions | Changes in conversion rates, revenue, transaction values |
| Sources | Performance shifts in traffic sources, new sources detected |
| Campaigns | ROAS changes, budget anomalies, CTR variations |
| Technical | Pixel errors, data delays, tracking issues |

[Learn more about anomaly detection →](./anomaly-detection)

### AI Assistant

Ask LENS questions about your data in natural language:

- *"Why did conversions drop last week?"*
- *"Which campaigns are performing best this month?"*
- *"Compare traffic from Google vs Facebook"*

LENS uses advanced LLMs (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini, DeepSeek) to analyze your data and provide contextual answers.

[Learn more about the AI assistant →](./ai-assistant)

### Automated Reports

Receive proactive insights without lifting a finger:

- **Real-time alerts** - Instant notifications when anomalies are detected
- **Weekly executive summary** - Key metrics, trends, and recommendations
- **Custom reports** - Schedule reports for specific metrics or segments

[Learn more about LENS reports →](./reports)
