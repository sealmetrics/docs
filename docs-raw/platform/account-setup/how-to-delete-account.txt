# How to Delete a Site

**Warning:**

## Steps

1. In the left sidebar, expand **Organization** and click **Sites** (`/settings/sites`).
2. Find the site you want to delete in the **Your Sites** list.
3. Click the **trash icon** on the right side of the site row.
4. Confirm in the browser dialog ("Are you sure you want to delete this site?").

The site will be removed from your list immediately.

---

Only the organization **Owner** can delete sites — Admins and Members cannot. If you don't see the delete button, contact your organization owner.
