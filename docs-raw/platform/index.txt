# Platform

URL: https://docs.sealmetrics.com/platform

# Platform Overview

The **Platform** section covers everything about running Sealmetrics day-to-day: how to configure your account, manage users and organizations, tune tracking and attribution settings, and administer billing.

## What you can configure

- **Account setup** — timezone, domains, multi-account structure. Start with [Add a new account](/platform/account-setup/how-to-add-new-account) and [Change timezone](/platform/account-setup/how-to-change-timezone).
- **[Organizations](/platform/organizations)** — multi-site and multi-team organization structure.
- **User management** — [Add a new user](/platform/user-management/how-to-add-new-user), [Roles management](/platform/user-management/roles-management), [Authorized IPs](/platform/user-management/authorized-ips), and [password changes](/platform/user-management/change-password).
- **Tracking & attribution** — fine-tune attribution with [Bypass POS or referrer](/platform/tracking-and-attribution-settings/bypass-pos-or-referrer).
- **[Platform settings](/platform/settings)** — integrations, tracking defaults, advanced controls, and LLM access.

## Related areas

- **[Billing](/billing)** — plans, invoices, and payment methods.
- **Security & privacy** — data handling, retention, and compliance posture. See [adblocker bypass](/security-privacy/adblocker-bypass) and [first-party tracking](/implementation/tracker/first-party).
- **[API](/api)** — programmatic access to platform resources.
