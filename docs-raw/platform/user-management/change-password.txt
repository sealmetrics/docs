# How to Change Your Password

Follow these steps to update your password securely.

## 1. Open Your Profile

Click on your username in the top-right corner and select **My profile**.

![Change Password Step 1](/img/change-password-1.png)

## 2. Update Your Password

Inside the **User** section, update:

- Password  
- Password confirmation  

Click **Update** to save your changes.

---

If you cannot access your account, you can request a reset link from the login screen.
