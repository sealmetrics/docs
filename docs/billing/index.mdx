## Choosing the Right Plan

### Research (€199/month)

**Best for:** Small businesses, startups, single-website tracking

✅ Perfect if you:
- Track one website or app
- Have under 500K monthly pageviews
- Need basic analytics and attribution
- Want to try privacy-first analytics

### Guard (€399/month)

**Best for:** Growing businesses, agencies, multi-site tracking

✅ Perfect if you:
- Manage multiple websites or clients
- Need advanced anomaly detection
- Want real-time alerts and reporting
- Require BigQuery integration

### Growth (€599/month)

**Best for:** Enterprises, high-traffic sites, advanced needs

✅ Perfect if you:
- Have high-traffic websites (10M+ pageviews)
- Need unlimited team access
- Require custom integrations
- Want dedicated support and SLA

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately:
- **Upgrades:** Prorated charge for remaining billing period
- **Downgrades:** Credit applied to future invoices

### Is there a free trial?

Yes, all plans include a 14-day free trial with full features. No credit card required to start.

### What happens if I exceed my pageview limit?

You'll receive notifications at 80% and 100% usage. If you exceed:
- Service continues uninterrupted
- Overage charged at €0.20 per 10,000 pageviews
- Option to upgrade to avoid overages

### Do you offer discounts?

- **Annual billing:** Save ~17% (2 months free)
- **Nonprofits:** 50% discount (contact us)
- **Startups:** Special pricing available (contact us)

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime with no penalties. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
