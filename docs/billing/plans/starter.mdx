## Getting Started

1. **Sign up** at [my.sealmetrics.com](https://my.sealmetrics.com/register)
2. Start your **14-day free trial**
3. Install the tracking pixel
4. Choose Starter plan before trial ends

Your card will not be charged during the 14-day trial period.

## Upgrading

Upgrade to Growth, Scale, Pro, or Enterprise anytime:

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select new plan
4. Confirm upgrade

Upgrades take effect immediately. You'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing period.
