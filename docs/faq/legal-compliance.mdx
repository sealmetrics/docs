# Legal & Compliance FAQ

## Is Sealmetrics GDPR compliant?

Yes.  
Because we do not track individuals and do not collect personal data.

---

## Does Sealmetrics require consent?

No.  
We comply with CNIL and AEPD guidelines for cookieless, consentless measurement.

---

## Do you store IP addresses?

No.  
IP addresses are not stored or used for identification.

---

## Has Sealmetrics undergone audits?

Yes.  
Multiple technical and legal audits validate our privacy-first architecture.
