## Dashboard Overview

When you open the dashboard, it displays key metrics at a glance:

- **Visits**  
- **Page views**  
- **Conversions**  
- **Revenuerevenue**  
- **Micro-conversions**

These indicators give you a complete picture of your traffic and performance.  
A **cost calculator** and **conversion rate** widget provide quick financial insights, while comparisons with the last **30 days** show trends in your metrics.

---

## Micro-Conversions

SealMetrics can measure any type of **micro-conversion**, including:

- Lead submissions  
- Add-to-cart actions  
- Newsletter sign-ups  
- Logins  
- Checkouts  
- Scroll depth  
- Any custom event you define  

Each micro-conversion can be compared with the previous **31-day period**, displaying **green** indicators for growth and **red** for decline.

---

## Conversion Tracking

At the **conversion level**, SealMetrics measures final actions such as completed purchases, sign-ups, or goal completions.  
These are displayed alongside micro-conversion metrics to help you understand your full funnel performance.

---

## Business Growth Insights

Visually, you can track how your business evolves over time — both in conversions and micro-conversions.  
The dashboard provides an at-a-glance visualization of:

- **Conversion evolution**
- **Micro-conversion performance**
- **Traffic trends**

This makes it easy to spot growth patterns or areas needing improvement.

---

## Traffic Analysis

The dashboard also includes a detailed **traffic graph**, showing how visits evolve daily.  
You can easily identify:

- Which **mediums** generate the most traffic and sales  
- The **traffic sources** that convert best  
- How campaigns perform over time, with **green** for growth and **red** for decline

---

## Page Performance

At the **page-view level**, you can explore:

- Which URLs attract the most entry traffic  
- Which pages generate the highest total views  
- How performance varies by **device**, **operating system**, and **country**

This granular visibility allows you to understand exactly where visitors engage most with your content.

---

## Summary

The **SealMetrics Dashboard** offers a full 360º view of your analytics:

- Complete KPIs: visits, conversions, micro-conversions  
- Automatic period comparison (30–31 days)  
- Visual trend tracking with growth indicators  
- Source and page-level breakdowns  
- Fully privacy-compliant, cookieless data collection  

> With SealMetrics, you get actionable insights — **without cookies, without consent banners, and without data loss.**
