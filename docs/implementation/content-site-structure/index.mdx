# Content & Site Structure

Learn how to organize and track your site content effectively with SealMetrics. This section explains how to structure your analytics data to gain meaningful insights about how visitors navigate and consume your content.

Proper content organization helps you understand which sections of your website perform best, identify popular content categories, and optimize your site architecture. Content grouping enables you to analyze performance across logical sections rather than individual pages, making it easier to spot trends and make data-driven decisions about your content strategy.
