## About SealMetrics

SealMetrics enables **100% traffic tracking without cookies or consent banners**, compliant with GDPR and CNIL guidelines.  
All data is processed and stored in the EU, ensuring full privacy and accuracy.

> **SealMetrics** — analytics without consent, without data loss, and without compromise.
