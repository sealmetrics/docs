# How to Add Users to the Breakfast Report  
*(and Enable/Disable It)*

The **Breakfast Report** is a daily email that delivers the most important performance metrics straight to your inbox, including:

- Total Revenue  
- Completed Conversions  
- Entrances / Sessions  
- Top Traffic Sources  

---

## Steps to Set Up the Breakfast Report

<img
  src="/img/platform/breakfast-report.png"
  alt="Breakfast report"
/>

### **1. Go to Settings**
Open your dashboard → click the **gear icon** → choose **Breakfast Report**.

### **2. Review What’s Included**
A full description is shown on the right-hand side.

### **3. Enable or Disable**
Use the toggle to turn the report **On** or **Off**.

### **4. Add Email Recipients**
Add one or multiple email addresses separated by commas.

### **5. Save Your Changes**
Click **Update** to apply settings.
