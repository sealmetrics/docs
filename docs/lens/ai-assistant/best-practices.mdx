# Best Practices for LENS AI

Get more accurate, actionable insights by following these best practices when asking questions.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Don't Ask Multiple Unrelated Questions

‚ùå *"What's our traffic? Also, what's the best performing campaign? And can you show me revenue by country?"*

‚úÖ Ask one topic at a time, then follow up.

### Don't Assume LENS Knows Your Goals

‚ùå *"Is this good?"*

‚úÖ *"Is a 2.3% conversion rate good compared to our historical average?"*

‚úÖ *"How does our 2.3% conversion rate compare to last year?"*

### Don't Forget Time Zones

If your data spans multiple time zones:

‚úÖ *"What was traffic on January 10th PST?"*

‚úÖ *"Show me hourly traffic in UTC"*

### Don't Overlook Segments

Generic questions get generic answers:

‚ùå *"Why is bounce rate high?"*

‚úÖ *"Why is bounce rate high for mobile users from Facebook ads landing on the homepage?"*
