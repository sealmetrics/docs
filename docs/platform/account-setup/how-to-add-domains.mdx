# How to Add Domains in Your Account

Sealmetrics defines an entrance as a click that meets one of two conditions:

- An empty referrer  
- A referrer from a different domain than your own  

If the referrer is from a different domain (e.g., facebook.com â†’ yoursite.com), Sealmetrics counts the visit as a new entrance.
