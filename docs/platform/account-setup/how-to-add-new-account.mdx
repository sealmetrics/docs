# How to Add a New Account

Follow these steps to add a new account:

### 1. Open the **Monitor** menu and click **Accounts**  
<img src="/img/account-setup/add-account-step1.png" alt="Add new account step 1"/>

### 2. Select **Create New Account**  
<img src="/img/account-setup/add-account-step2.png" alt="Add new account step 2"/>

### 3. Fill in all required information  
<img src="/img/account-setup/add-account-step3.png" alt="Add new account step 3"/>

### 4. Set your time zone  
<img src="/img/account-setup/add-account-step4.png" alt="Add new account step 4"/>

### 5. Click **Submit**  
<img src="/img/account-setup/add-account-step5.png" alt="Add new account step 5"/>

---

Your new account will now appear in your Accounts list.
