# How to Change Your Time Zone

### 1. Go to **Accounts**
<img src="/img/account-setup/change-timezone-step1.png" alt="Change timezone step 1"/>

### 2. Edit the account  
<img src="/img/account-setup/change-timezone-step2.png" alt="Change timezone step 2"/>

### 3. Select a new Time Zone and click **Update**  
<img src="/img/account-setup/change-timezone-step3.png" alt="Change timezone step 3"/>

By default, accounts use UTC until you modify this setting.
