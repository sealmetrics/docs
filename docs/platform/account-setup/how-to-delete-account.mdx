# How to Delete a Site

:::warning
Deleting a site permanently removes all its tracking data. This action cannot be undone.
:::

## Steps

1. Go to **Settings â†’ Sites** in the sidebar.
2. Find the site you want to delete in the **Your Sites** list.
3. Click the **trash icon** on the right side of the site row.
4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears.

The site will be removed from your list immediately.

---

Only users with the appropriate permissions (Owner or Admin role) can delete sites. If you don't see the delete button, contact your organization owner.
