# How to Change Your Password

Follow these steps to update your password securely.

## 1. Open Your Profile

In the sidebar, open the **My Account** group and click **Profile**.

The Profile page shows your account information and a **Change Password** section.

## 2. Update Your Password

In the **Change Password** section, fill in:

- **Current Password**
- **New Password** (must be at least 12 characters)
- **Confirm New Password**

Click **Change Password** to save your changes. A confirmation message appears once the password has been updated.

:::tip Two-Factor Authentication
You can add an extra layer of security by enabling two-factor authentication from **My Account → Security**, which is also where you manage your active sessions.
:::

---

If you cannot access your account, you can request a reset link from the login screen.
