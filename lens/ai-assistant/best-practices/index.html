‚ùå "What's our traffic? Also, what's the best performing campaign? And can you show me revenue by country?"

‚ùå "Is this good?"

‚úÖ "Is a 2.3% conversion rate good compared to our historical average?"

‚úÖ "How does our 2.3% conversion rate compare to last year?"

Don't Forget Time Zones‚Äã

‚úÖ "What was traffic on January 10th PST?"

Don't Overlook Segments‚Äã

‚ùå "Why is bounce rate high?"
