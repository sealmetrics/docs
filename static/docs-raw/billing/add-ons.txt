# Add-ons & Extras

All plans already include unlimited websites and unlimited users. The add-ons below cover AI capacity and Enterprise custom needs.

## Seal AI Private

Sealmetrics' EU-hosted, platform-managed AI for [LENS](/lens/) — no API key needed, no prompt retention, processed entirely in the EU.

| | |
|---|---|
| Price | €299/month billed annually upfront (€3,588/year) · €358.80/month on monthly contracts |
| Included quota | 5M tokens per calendar month |
| Extra capacity | 5M token packs at €358.80 each (1–10 per checkout, **never expire**) |
| Availability | Add-on on Growth · **Included** in Scale and Enterprise · Not available on Free |

Full details: **[Seal AI Private](/billing/seal-ai-private)**.

**Note:**
Instead of (or alongside) Seal AI Private, you can connect your **own** Anthropic, OpenAI, DeepSeek, or Gemini API key. With BYOK your AI usage is billed directly by that provider and Sealmetrics does not meter it. See [LLM Providers](/platform/settings/llm).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I pay Sealmetrics extra for AI usage?

Only if you use **Seal AI Private** (the managed EU provider): it's a paid add-on on Growth and included in Scale/Enterprise, with a 5M token/month quota and optional token packs. If you use **your own LLM key (BYOK)** instead, Sealmetrics doesn't charge or meter anything — your usage is billed by your provider.
