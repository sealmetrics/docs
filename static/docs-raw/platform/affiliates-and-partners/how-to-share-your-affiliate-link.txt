# How to Share Your Affiliate Link

Sealmetrics includes a simple affiliate system that lets you earn recurring commissions by referring new paid users.  
Follow the steps below to generate and share your unique affiliate link.

---

## Steps to Create Your Affiliate Link

1. Go to **Setup**
2. Select **Affiliate**
3. Enter the name you'd like to include in your affiliate link
4. Click **Create**
5. Share the generated link

---

## How Commissions Work

Every time someone becomes a **Paid User** through your affiliate link:

- You earn **20% commission**
- The referred user receives **10% off**

This makes the program mutually beneficial and encourages long-term referrals.

---

## Where to View Your Commissions

You can access all your commission details in the **Affiliate Control Panel**:

From there, you can monitor:

- Monthly earned commissions  
- Pending and approved payments  
- Performance of your referral links
